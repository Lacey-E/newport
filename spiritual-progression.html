My Journey of Integration

"To use my God-given talents in technology to serve others, lead with integrity, and create solutions that benefit humanity while honoring my Creator."

This journey has fundamentally transformed my understanding of how my spiritual and professional lives can and should work together. My goals have shifted from asking "What can I achieve?" to "How can I serve?" I've learned that true leadership requires character, not just competence, and that spiritual disciplines like daily prayer enhance professional performance by improving focus, creativity, and empathy. My aim is to be a professional who leads with integrity, serves others, and uses technology to solve meaningful problems.

Action Plan & Goals
